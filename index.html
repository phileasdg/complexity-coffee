What is Complexity Coffee?

Complexity Coffee is a community-driven virtual series from Santa Fe Institute alums, presented in partnership with SFI. We are dedicated to fostering discussion and sharing new ideas in the field of complexity science.

Our goal is to create an accessible, global forum for exploring the diverse topics, methods, and applications of complexity. Whether you're a seasoned researcher or just complexity-curious, grab a cup of coffee and join the conversation.

Our Event Formats

We host several types of events, each designed for a different purpose.

Guest Talks

In-depth lectures from leading researchers and guest lecturers on foundational and cutting-edge topics in complexity science. Followed by an extended Q&A session.
