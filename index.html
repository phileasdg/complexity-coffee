What is Complexity Coffee?

Complexity Coffee is a community-driven virtual series from Complexity Global School alums, presented in partnership with SFI. We are dedicated to fostering discussion and sharing new ideas in the field of complexity science.

This initiative is organized by the alumni of the Complexity Global School (CGS). Since 2023, CGS has engaged students from 30+ countries. Complexity Coffee connects this growing alumni network with leading researchers in complexity science through talks, interactive workshops, and collaborative opportunities.

Our goal is to create an accessible, global forum for exploring the diverse topics, methods, and applications of complexity. Whether you're a seasoned researcher or just complexity-curious, grab a cup of coffee and join the conversation.
