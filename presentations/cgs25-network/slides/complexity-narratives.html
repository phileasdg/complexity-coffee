Inaugural event

Coal, storytelling, and just transitions in the Caribbean

Dr. Cardoso Diaz Wednesday, July 15 at 10:00 AM Colombia Time

A narrative-driven exploration of coal extraction and its phaseout in the Colombian Caribbean. Grounded in reflections from the Transition Away from Fossil Fuels Conference in Santa Marta, this inaugural talk connects local territorial impacts to epistemologies of the South, just transitions, and global policy discussions.
